FACADES

The first reason for insulating a building is the savings resulting from energy consumption for heating the building, reaching up to 30-40%.

The second reason is the visual effect of the building's facade. Along with the insulation of the walls come thin-coat plasters of different weights, rustications, cornices, a full palette of colors that give the building an original image and character.

The right building insulation guarantees the optimal temperature of the apartment in summer and winter. A well-constructed thermo-modernization can save up to a few hundred pounds of energy for air conditioning or heating. The thermal insulation of the building walls improves the climate and the warmth of the rooms.

INSULATION AND ADAPTATION OF THE ATTIC

It depends on good roof insulation not only to keep us warm!

Lack of or poor insulation in the attic can cause a number of complications, including the wood roof trusses becoming moldy, lowering their structural performance. Proper attic insulation determines whether we will not have problems with moisture - not only in the attic rooms.

Our experience in the construction industry allows us to advise you on the choice of materials for roofs, walls, elevations and finishing work. We also offer you assistance and advice in solving specific problems. Our advantage is our individual approach to customers and affordable prices.

DECORATIVE PLASTERS

Decorative plasters allow you to create unique solutions and something unique. They turn every square metre of an area into a work of art.

The use of suitable materials and application techniques makes it possible to create old structures, classic wall decorations, imitations of structures that occur in nature (e. g. rock, minerals, earth, wood, etc. ), and allows the restoration of surfaces that occur around us, such as architectural concrete or rust phenomena.

The only limitation is your imagination!

In our projects we always use high-quality products from the well-known company ATLAS. These products are characterized by high durability and ease of use.
